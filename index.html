What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a cryptocurrency that was meant for a new form of currency to be free from the current market and a full-on global currency. In it's infancy, people have judged it and said that it is not a currency and that since it is digital, that it can be easily manupulated which in some cases could be true. It however is inevitable that it will become a form currency that will be widely accepted globally and there is no government that can stop it.

History

Bitcoin was invented by an unknown person or group of people using the name Satoshi Nakamoto and was released as open-source software in 2009. Bitcoins are created as a reward for a process known as mining. They can be exchanged for other currencies, products, and services.

Controversy

This then makes the question, if I purchase a cup of Starbucks Coffee today at $3, is that same cup considered $6 tomorrow? These are the real reasons why it needs to be regulated so price manipulation isn't a factor and can be used in increments or perhaps consider it like "Digital Gold" since no one in current day pays anything in Gold.
